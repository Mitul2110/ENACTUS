enactus

Manipal University Jaipur

We, at Enactus MU-J, believe in the unyielding power of socially conscious entrepreneurship to create a more sustainable world through purpose-driven projects, spreading awareness and educating minds above all else. We consider ourselves advocates of change, willing to tackle all social issues by replacing them with innovatively crafted alternatives, leveraging our best talents and perspectives. By undertaking these challenges as a social responsibility of each individual collaborating with us, we envision ourselves rising above the confines of the university to target a global audience, propelling our projects as change makers thereby strengthening a community.

Our Projects

Our projects aim at empowering people in need. We use business and economic concepts and an entrepreneurial approach to improve the quality of life and standard of living of the diverse communities that we work with. Enactus SRCC has taken up 16 projects till date, of which 12 have been successfully completed and 4 are continuing. The continuing four projects are Project Asbah, Project Virasat, Project Leher and Project Amal
